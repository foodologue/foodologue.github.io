A Personal Restaurant Catalogue for Bonn and Cologne

Other Cities:

Berlin

  1. Takenori: Japanese restaurant on Simon-Dach-Straße. I relished their Katsu Curry and would often spend my first paycheck as an intern there!

Budapest

  1. VakVarjú Restaurant: Located on the West of Danube in Pest, I really liked the Madártej (Bird's Milk), a dessert made with egg whites! Very close to the St. Stephen's Basilica. I highly reccommend trying out the Túrógombóc (cottage cheese with sour creme), but I cannot recall the restaurant I had them. The food was authentic according to a couple of locals.
  2. Espresso Embassy: I enjoyed the coffee there as well as the general ambience. Thought I might mention this for future reference!
