Other Cities:

Bergamo

  1. La Gasthaus Bistrot: My flight landed close to midnight and this was one of the places open (till 3 am!). I have never been more grateful for a meal. Their Brasato di Manzo made me feel immensely thankful for everything in life.

Berlin

  1. Takenori: Japanese restaurant on Simon-Dach-Straße. I relished their Katsu Curry and would often spend my first paycheck as an intern there!

Budapest

  1. VakVarjú Restaurant: Located on the West of Danube in Pest, I really liked the Madártej (Bird's Milk), a dessert made with egg whites! Very close to the St. Stephen's Basilica. I highly recommend trying out the Túrógombóc (cottage cheese with sour cream), but I cannot recall the restaurant I had them. The food was authentic according to a couple of locals.

  2. Espresso Embassy: I enjoyed the coffee there as well as the general ambience. Thought I might mention this for future reference.
