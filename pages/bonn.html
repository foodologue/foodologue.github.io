A Personal Restaurant Catalogue for Bonn and Cologne

Bonn

Bonn is not a particularly large city but it does reveal the odd gem from time to time!

Restaurants in Bonn

  1. Roha Café & Restaurant: Fantastic Ethiopian food! There aren't so many Ethiopian places in Bonn but luckily, this one is great!
  2. Café Nova: I primarily treat it as a brunch place. I have been to the one on Bottlerplatz and I quite like their Cortado.
  3. Mukam Restaurant: I really like the dumplings here as well as the mussel salad. Uyghur cuisine.

Wishlist for Bonn

  1. China Thai Imbiss Jumbo: They have two different menus.
